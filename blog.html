Starting out

by The Dentist

Posted on July 04, 2014 at 14:05 PM

A long woven dream of having a dental practice of his own, Dr Rikki Dhody has finally managed to open one. It all started from a feeling to make a difference in the society. From opening a dental practice in his back garden shed to now, opening a practice at Liskeard Road, Park Hall, not too far from his home.

Dentistry has always been his passion, he loves his job. With his keen interest and calm and composed nature, patients love him and want to be seen by him.

To open a new Private Dental Practice with a lot of resilience was not easy for him. But his passion for his work drove him so far. He took those stumbling blocks in his stride and carried onto make his dream a reality.

The reception gives you a very warm feeling when you enter it, a feeling of being there; not like a typical surgery. The staff are super friendly and welcoming and to top it all, they have the patience to listen to what patients are saying.

The practice has a potential to have 3 surgeries, but at the moment since its a new practice, only one surgery is in operation.

The core values of the practice are impressive, and the staff emphasises on them:

  • Honest staff
  • Convenient timings (8-11am and 4-8pm) and 24 hour (emergency service)
