Treatments

Examination with X-rays

A regular check up helps spot problems before they become too deep:

  • Spot decay before it hits the nerve and you'll save yourself plenty of pain and money
  • Interrupt gum disease before it causes irreversible tooth sensitivity, movement and loss
  • Check for mouth cancer because early diagnosis is the biggest difference between life and death

Clean (scale) and polish

Performed by a dentist or hygienist, this:

  • Stops bad breath
  • Keeps teeth healthy
  • Prevents gum disease and bleeding gums, keeping your teeth for longer

Fillings

Fillings help replace broken parts of teeth, stops decay from having a place to stay and can even improve the appearance of your teeth. Talk to your dentist about the different types of filling you can have. White, metal, cement and in between.

Root fillings

When the nerve inside the tooth is irritated or infection has taken hold. Clean the root canal fully and you will save the tooth rather than having to take it out.

Crowns

When a filling isn't strong enough, consider a crown. We do all types of crown. The most frequent decision to have a porcelain or a porcelain and metal crown.

Extractions

When a tooth is better out than in, we will make sure that it is fully numb before wobbling the tooth out.

Veneers

For changing the shape of teeth for a better smile

Replace missing teeth

We provide many options to replace missing teeth, from simple plastic dentures to metal or flexible dentures, and standard bridges to 'sticky' bridges

Tooth whitening

To improve the colour and youth of your teeth. We can do tooth whitening in surgery and at home.
