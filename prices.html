Prices

We can match NHS prices.

Your check-up, X-rays, clean (scale) and polish All in at £18.50
All of the fillings that you need All in at £50.50
All of the crowns or dentures that you need All in at £219

These are exactly what you pay on the NHS and you receive the same guarantees (12 months).

Note that, exactly as is with the NHS, these prices apply for one course of treatment only and will provide the most basic treatment to achieve a healthy mouth.


Special treatment

If you want white fillings on the back teeth, or additional quality such as crowns made entirely of a stronger porcelain, or a metal based (and therefore stronger) or flexible (and therefore better fitting) denture, the fees will be different.
